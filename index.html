About

Stefan Troschka (*1986) is a media artist from Hamburg/Germany. He did his B.Sc. in Media technology and subsequently finished his M.A. in Timebased media/sound-vision with the focus on experimental sound design and 3D audio in 2013 at the Hamburg University of Applied Sciences. He is currently enrolled in the Masters program in Multimedia composition at the Hamburg University of Music and Drama.
