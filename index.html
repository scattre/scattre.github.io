About

Stefan Troschka (*1986), media artist and musician, lives and works in Hamburg. In his installations and electronic pieces, experimental electronic sounds and generative video projections expand into walk-in multimedia spaces. His works have been performed at Transitions Festival (CCRMA), Greatest Hits - Festival for Contemporary Music, Klub Katarakt - Festival for Experimental Music, Next Generation (ZKM) and Blurred Edges Festival.

Fields of activity: Electroacoustic composition, generative video, sensor technology and interaction
